About Us

The Silverware Factory was founded in 1950 by John Smith. John was a master silversmith, and he had a passion for creating beautiful and functional silverware. The factory has been in operation ever since, and we are proud to continue John's legacy.

We are committed to using the finest materials and to creating the highest quality silverware. We also believe in giving back to the community, and we support a number of local charities.
